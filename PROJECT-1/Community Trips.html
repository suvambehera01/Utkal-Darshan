Community Stay in Odisha

10 Nights & 11 Days

Bhubaneswar – Dhenkanal – Daringbadi – Kandhamal – Sathi Ghara – Bhubaneswar

Highlights

Community Stay India, Responsible Tourism, Eco Tourism Odisha, Livelihood Tourism, NGO Tourism, Village Tourism, Local Cuisine, Cultural Programs, Tribal Tourism.

Itinerary

Day 01: Bhubaneswar Arrival

Arrival at Bhubaneswar Airport/Railway Station, meet & greet, transfer to hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 02: Bhubaneswar Sightseeing – Dhenkanal Palace

Visit temples and Tribal Museum. Drive to Dhenkanal Palace. Cultural evening.

Day 03: Dhenkanal Palace

Visit tribal villages, Joronda Temple, and local crafts. Overnight at Dhenkanal.

Day 04: Dhenkanal – Daringbadi

Drive to Daringbadi. Visit pine forest and coffee gardens.

Day 05: Daringbadi Tour

Explore nature and tribal culture. Stay at Daringbadi.

Day 06: Daringbadi – Belghar

Meet Kutia Kondh tribe. Wildlife and cultural night.
